About Us

Cat World is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of street cats. We believe every cat deserves love and respect.

Our mission is to raise public awareness about cats through education, adoption services, and medical care, ensuring a better life for these mysterious companions.

Founded by cat lovers, our team is composed of veterinary professionals, volunteers, and advocates working together to make a difference in the lives of cats, both locally and globally.
